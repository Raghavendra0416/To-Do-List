To-Do Lists

ABOUT:

Welcome to my little corner of the internet!
I built this tool for a simple reason: We all carry around too much mental clutter. This app was built to be a simple, reliable container for all those "I need to remember to..." moments. Its designed to be lightweight and fast, allowing you to dump your ideas quickly so you can get back to doing what you love.
I was tired of writing important tasks on sticky notes, only to find them stuck to the bottom of my coffee mug (or lost entirely) five minutes later. I needed a digital home for my thoughts that was simple, clean, and always there when I needed it.
Whether you're planning your weekly grocery run, breaking down a complex coding project, or just mapping out your life goals, I hope this little app helps you declutter your mind and stay on track.
Feel free to add as many tasks as you like.
How it Works:
This is a lightweight task management tool.
Add: Type your task and hit Enter to add it to the list.
Track: Click the item to mark it as completed.
Clean: Use the "Delete" button to remove items permanently.
Save: Your list is automatically saved to your browser's local storage.
